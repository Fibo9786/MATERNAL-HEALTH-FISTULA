Services

Fistula Kenya Initiative offers a wide variety of services which include;

  • Door to door Fistula campaigns where we offer Fistula related information
  • Local Health care Facility follow ups where we seek to find and locate those with fistula
  • Collection of donor funds which helps us sponsor corrective surgey.
  • Follow up after corrective surgery and awareness creation
